Welcome to Tru Kreations.My journey with beads started many years ago.Each piece is unique and handmade by me. I use gemstones, pearls,glass & metal beads in my designs. Now I offer these kreations to the public.

I am drawn towards gemstones as they are earths treasures that have been used by many civilizations. It is said that gemstones have extraordinary effects in the realms of the physical, spiritual and emotional planes. In many cultures people have associated certain gemstones with certain months in the calendar. In today's world we believe that a certain stone that correlates with the month of our birth is meaningful in our lives.

I love working with gemstones because of the symbology, power, preciousness and beauty. I hope you like my designs. Please contact me if you have questions or comments.
